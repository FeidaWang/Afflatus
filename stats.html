Methodology (honest small print)
  • Wilson score interval instead of the normal approximation — the standard choice for small-n binomial proportions.
  • Exact two-sided binomial test against p=0.5: with n this small, "beats a coin flip" is a claim the data cannot yet support either way.
  • = mean (stated probability − outcome)²; lower is better. Skill score BSS = 1 − BS/0.25 compares against an always-50% forecaster.
  • Exact-scoreline hits require the outcome to be correct first — a string coincidence with the winner called wrong does not count.
  • Nothing is re-scored after the fact: this page reads the same public JSON the live pages rendered from.

Scope of this archive
  • Headline resolved/hit-rate/exact-score counts are the full-tournament totals from the live page's own running tally — never recomputed, just read.
  • Per-match charts, Brier score, calibration and the log table are limited to the Round of 16 through the Final (16 matches) — the only matches whose stated confidence and reasoning text survived in the data file.
